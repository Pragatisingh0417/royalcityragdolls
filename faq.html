Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about reserving and adopting a kitten

To reserve a kitten, a deposit is required. If the kittens are not yet born, selection of kitten(s) is done in the order deposits are received. Deposits are non-refundable but can be transferred to another litter in case of an emergency.

You can contact us directly by email, phone, or Facebook, or by filling out our adoption form. We are happy to guide you through the process and answer any questions you may have.

Kittens are ready to leave for their forever homes at 8 weeks of age, after completing their veterinary check and receiving their first vaccination.

We are a spay and neuter cattery. Your kitten will receive the second vaccination, spay/neuter surgery, microchip, and post-operative care. All procedures are completed by our trusted veterinarian.

Yes, we offer kitten care from 8 to 12 weeks for families who live at a distance. This service is available for an additional cost.

Each kitten goes home with:
  • TICA registration form
  • Vitamins
  • Sample food
  • Toys
  • A blanket that smells like mom

Royal City Ragdolls is a reputable breeder of Ragdoll cats in Canada, dedicated to raising healthy, affectionate, and well-socialized kittens with exceptional care and ethics.
