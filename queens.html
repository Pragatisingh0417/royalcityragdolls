What are Ragdoll Queens?

Ragdoll Queens, like their male counterparts (Ragdoll Kings), are a breed of cats renowned for their large size and gentle demeanor. Originating in the 1960s through selective breeding efforts by :contentReference[oaicite:0]{index=0} in Riverside, California, Ragdoll Queens share the breed’s characteristic traits of plush fur, striking blue eyes, and a relaxed disposition.

Key Features

Appearance

Ragdoll Queens exhibit a regal appearance with their large, muscular build and semi-long, silky coat that comes in various colours and patterns. They typically have darker points on their ears, face, paws, and tail, complemented by a lighter body colour. Their almond-shaped blue eyes contribute to their gentle and captivating gaze.

Temperament

Ragdoll Queens are known for their affectionate and laid-back nature, earning them a reputation as excellent companions for families and individuals alike. They enjoy human company and are often described as “people-oriented,” forming strong bonds with their owners and adapting well to indoor environments.

History and Origins

The history of Ragdoll Queens mirrors that of Ragdoll Kings, tracing back to Ann Baker’s pioneering breeding efforts in the 1960s. By crossing Josephine, a white domestic longhaired cat with Siamese-like markings, with other cats of unknown origin, Ann Baker aimed to create a breed with a gentle temperament and striking appearance.

The breed quickly gained recognition by major cat associations like :contentReference[oaicite:1]{index=1} (TICA) and the :contentReference[oaicite:2]{index=2} (CFA), solidifying its place as a cherished feline companion worldwide.

Types of Ragdoll Queens

  • colourpoint: Classic pattern with darker points contrasted against a lighter body.
  • Mitted: White paw markings, often with a white chin and belly.
  • Bicolour: Extensive white markings with an inverted “V” on the face.
  • Lynx (Tabby) Point: Tabby striping on points for a wild, elegant look.
  • Tortie (Tortoiseshell): A mix of two or more colours in random patches.
  • Solid: Uniform coat colour without visible markings.

Each type of Ragdoll Queen offers its own allure, allowing prospective owners to choose a cat that matches their preferences and lifestyle.

Why Choose a Ragdoll Queen as a Pet?

  • Elegance and Beauty: Luxurious fur, striking blue eyes, and graceful demeanor.
  • Affectionate Nature: Gentle, loving temperament and strong bonds with owners.
  • Companionship: Loyal, people-oriented cats ideal for family life.
